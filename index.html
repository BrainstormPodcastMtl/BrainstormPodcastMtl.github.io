Brainstorm: Exploring Minds & Behaviours

Brainstorm: Exploring Minds and Behaviors is an accessible science podcast for lovers of psychology and neuroscience! Join us to listen to two Montreal-based graduate students sharing their passion for all things brain-related through invigorating interdisciplinary expert panel discussions on topics traditionally thought of as psychology and neuroscience. By interacting with other graduate students and professors from across Canada and beyond, Brainstorm hopes to build a community where anyone interested in psychology and neuroscience can learn more by simply tuning in.

Across Fields

Examining topics in psychology with experts from a variety of fields.

Across Universities

Discussions with experts from Universities across North America.

For Everyone

Invigorating discussions intended for academics and the public alike.

"Brainstorm is nothing short of an attempt to develop a platform upon which open-science conversations can begin"
